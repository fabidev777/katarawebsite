Our Story

Katara Real Estate is the flagship company of our group of businesses. Our group of companies is engaged in multiple ranges of business establishments in the State of Qatar under strong leadership, with innovative thoughts and enterprising in business.


Our company was established in the year 2011 with the objective of making its own landmark in the field of real estate business. We were able to establish our credibility with valued customers over a period of time, making us proud to say that we are one of the largest real estate companies in the State of Qatar.


We are also one of the few Certified Real Estate Evaluators in the State of Qatar, meeting the latest regulations as per the Qatar government. We mainly deal with the construction of buildings and deals with the renting, managing, buying & selling of properties and buildings in a sophisticated manner.


We are planning of expanding our businesses with the latest state of the art technology (including Ai), in our various business activities in the State of Qatar and also by partnering with other Internationally reputed Real Estate Owners & Government bodies to have an active part & also to meet the surging demands, especially in Real Estate and related sectors, during the booming time of FIFA World Cup 2022 and Qatar National Vision 2030.


And Yes ! We support Qatar's delivery of Successful Qatar 2022 FIFA World Cup.
